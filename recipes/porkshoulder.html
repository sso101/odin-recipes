Pork Shoulder Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound> pork shoulder roast
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 table spoon Vegetable Oil
  • 1 large onion
  • cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Salt the pork shoulder
  2. Transfer pork shoulder pieces to cider mixture
  3. Raise heat to high and bring sauce to a simmer
